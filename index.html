Welcome to the portfolio of Filip Wennedahl

Hello there! I am Filip, a software developer with a bachelors degree in Computer and System Sciences. My education had a focus on game development and programming but did also include system- and web development. I'm very passionate about writing code but also creating well designed and stable systems.

Currently I am employed as an IT consultant at Kentor Sopra Steria, a company that provides services such as system development, QA testing, integration and application administration.

Because of my passion for games and the fast growing market of game development I use some of my spare time to both play and develop games. When I'm not spending time in front of a screen you will usually find me out roaming in nature, all to keep both a healthy physical and mental balance.

Feel free to check out my work, both under the "Work/Projects" and the "Code Samples" tab but also this website as a whole. Do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or are interested in my work.
